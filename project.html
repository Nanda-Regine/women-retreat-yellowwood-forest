Why WomenForest?

A gathering that is
Part Retreat.
Part Magic.

Imagine waking in a forest cathedral, moving your body on a wild beach at sunrise, sharing stories around a fire with women who truly see you. This isn't just a retreat — it's a regenerative re-set for your whole self.

🌲

Immerse in Nature

Indigenous forests, wild rivers, dramatic cliffs and breathtaking beaches — all yours to explore and be healed by.

💃

Soul Dance & Movement

Dance on the beach at sunrise. Move freely in the forest. Soul Dance is a practice of presence, joy and liberation for every body.

🤝

Sister Circle

Connect with women across all ages and backgrounds. Fireside ceremonies, shared meals, laughter and honest conversation.

✨

Co-Created & Facilitated

Not passive — you shape it. Workshops, creativity, land art and a closing ceremony planted in the earth.
